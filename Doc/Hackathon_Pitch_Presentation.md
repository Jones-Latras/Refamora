# Refamora: Hackathon Pitch & Speaker Script

> **Note for Presenters:** This deck is strictly aligned with the hackathon guidelines (7 slides total). You have **5 minutes total including the live demo**. Keep your slide explanations to about **2.5 to 3 minutes** so you have a solid **2 minutes** to show the live prototype.

---

## Slide 1: Problem Statement
* **Visual:** Refamora Logo and an impactful photo of agricultural waste burning.
* **Requirements Met:** What challenge? Why is it important for the Circular Economy?

🗣️ **Speaker Script (30 seconds):**
> "Good [morning/afternoon], judges. We are team [Your Team Name], and this is Refamora.
> 
> Right now, millions of tons of agricultural residue—like rice husk and corn stalks—are either burned or left to rot. Farmers see this as a disposal liability, causing massive carbon emissions. Meanwhile, eco-businesses are desperate for cheap, sustainable raw materials but have no way to find them locally. 
> 
> This is the biggest invisible hurdle to the Circular Economy: a complete lack of localized connection."

---

## Slide 2: Proposed Solution
* **Visual:** High-level app mockups showing the Farmer Dashboard and Buyer Map Feed sides.
* **Requirements Met:** Innovation proposed? How does it address the problem?

🗣️ **Speaker Script (20 seconds):**
> "Refamora is the solution. It is a mobile-first, distance-aware marketplace specifically designed to turn agricultural waste into local wealth. 
> 
> We provide farmers with a dead-simple platform to list their waste for sale. For buyers, we provide a map-based discovery feed to source sustainable materials nearby, drastically cutting down on logistics and transport emissions."

---

## Slide 3: AI Integration & Technology Used
* **Visual:** A simple architecture diagram showing the App client calling the Supabase backend edge functions, routed to "Local AI" or "Remote AI API".
* **Requirements Met:** Display ML, NLP, Generative AI, AI Decision Support, and Tech Stack.

🗣️ **Speaker Script (45 seconds):**
> "To make this marketplace frictionless for rural users, we heavily integrated AI. Under the hood, we use a hybrid stack of Local AI for fast, offline-capable inference, and Remote AI API for advanced reasoning.
>
> We leverage **Generative AI** and **NLP** via our 'Listing Copilot.' A farmer types 'rice husk 500 pesos', and the AI generates a complete, categorized listing instantly.
> 
> We use **AI Decision Support** through our 'Waste-to-Value Advisor', which analyzes the waste type and educates the farmer on its market potential, and **Computer Vision** to auto-check photo quality before publishing."

---

## Slide 4: Target Users & Beneficiaries
* **Visual:** Two distinct personas on screen. Left: "The Rural Farmer". Right: "The Eco-Business/Innovator".
* **Requirements Met:** Who are the users and how do they benefit?

🗣️ **Speaker Script (20 seconds):**
> "Our target users operate on two sides of the market.
>
> On the supply side, our beneficiaries are local, rural farmers looking to generate new income streams from what they used to throw away.
>
> On the demand side, we serve a variety of local businesses and innovators: mushroom growers needing substrate, livestock farmers sourcing feed and silage, composting facilities, bioethanol and biomass fuel producers, as well as textile and paper pulp manufacturers looking for reliable, sustainable, and local material sourcing, and even your neighbor who wants to start a small-scale composting business."

---

## Slide 5: Prototype Demonstration (Live Demo)
* **Visual:** Title Slide: "Refamora: Live Demo" - Switch to mirroring your phone or emulator.
* **Requirements Met:** Mandatory live prototype demo.

🗣️ **Demo Script (2 minutes):**
> *"Let’s see it action."*
> 1. **(Farmer side):** *"Here, I’m a farmer. I’ll open the Listing Copilot and just type 'got lots of coconut husks'. Instantly, the generative AI drafts a title, description, and categorizes it for me. Our Waste-to-Value Advisor also shows me who might buy this."*
> 2. **(Buyer side):** *"Switching to a buyer looking for materials, I can use the AI Natural Language Search. I type: 'show me coconut husks near me.' NLP interprets this and filters our interactive map instantly."*
> 3. **(Communication):** *"I tap the listing and can message the farmer safely right inside the app to coordinate pickup."*

## Slide 6: Impact on Circular Economy
* **Visual:** Metrics or icons representing: Reduced Emissions, Increased Income, Sustainable Sourcing.
* **Requirements Met:** Sustainable resource use, support communities.

🗣️ **Speaker Script (30 seconds):**
> "The impact here is measurable and immediate. 
> 
> By diverting waste from open burning into industrial use, we are drastically reducing localized carbon emissions and improving air quality. 
> 
> At the same time, we're injecting new money directly into farming communities, proving that sustainable resource use isn’t just good for the earth—it’s actually highly profitable for local governments and their citizens."

## Slide 7: Scalability and Sustainability
* **Visual:** A map showing initial target region, expanding outwards. A quick note on revenue model.
* **Requirements Met:** Scale beyond the region, long-term sustainability.

🗣️ **Speaker Script (30 seconds):**
> "Refamora is built to scale. Our tech stack using Expo and Supabase allows us to deploy anywhere. Our use of Local AI models ensures the app functions efficiently even in regions with poor internet connectivity, which is vital for rural scalability.
> 
> We will launch regionally first to build market density, but the platform is inherently borderless. Our ultimate goal is to standardize the trade of agricultural waste globally, making a zero-waste agricultural sector a sustainable, self-funding reality. 
> 
> Thank you, we are now ready for your questions."
